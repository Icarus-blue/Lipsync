How to Use This App

1Upload a Document

Navigate to the "Upload" section. Click "Choose File", select your document (CSV format), and click "Upload".

2View and Manage Q&A Pairs

In the "Q&A" section, you can view, add, edit, or delete Q&A pairs as needed.

3Train the Model

Go to the "Train Model" section and click "Train" to start the training process with your Q&A pairs.

4Ask Questions

In the "Ask" section, type your question and click "Submit" to get an answer from the trained model.

Need Help?

If you have any questions or encounter issues, please visit our "Contact Us" page for support.
